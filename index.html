Who am I?

Hi! I'm Susheel. I have 3+ years of experience in data analytics and consulting with focus on Business Intelligence. I have led led several projects in a client facing role including customer targeting and segmentation and product launch analytics. I am skilled in working with large datasets using Python, R, SAS, SQL and Excel, extracting relevant insights and presenting the same to stakeholders using tools like Powerpoint and Tableau.

I am driven to identify patterns and solve problems using data. Realizing the infinite possiblities present in data, I have always aimed to make data more accessible to the general user, implemeting ideas that help eliminate the tedious processes involved in obtaining data, and focus on extracting insights.
